# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Fellow developers and technical collaborators doing peer evaluation. They scan fast, read code, and judge taste from visual decisions. They want to know: how does this person think, what have they built, are they someone worth working with?

## Product Purpose

A personal profile page for Sh0ckWaveZero (MidSeeLee) — a Full Stack Developer. Exists to establish technical credibility and signal depth of craft to dev peers. Success: a dev lands on the page and immediately understands the skill level, aesthetic sensibility, and tech focus without reading a word of body copy.

## Brand Personality

Terminal-native. Precise. Dense.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing: no purple gradients, floating 3D blobs, hero metrics, or glassmorphism as decoration.
- Dribbble portfolio: no soft shadows, pastel gradients, over-rounded corners, or over-animated everything.
- Brutalist shock: no intentionally ugly high-contrast-outline aesthetic.

## Design Principles

1. **Signal over decoration** — every visual element communicates something; nothing is purely decorative.
2. **Density with hierarchy** — devs read dense interfaces; pack information but keep the type scale ruthlessly clear.
3. **Technical credibility IS the aesthetic** — the interface being well-built IS the personality. No gradient text, no blobs.
4. **Precision in the details** — craft shows in spacing, alignment, and rhythm, not in visual gimmicks.
5. **Earned expressiveness** — color and motion only when they carry meaning, not mood.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. 4.5:1 text contrast minimum, keyboard navigable, screen reader compatible. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
